Remake Guarantee

Our standard is perfection. Nothing less.

Because every NOIRID piece is crafted specifically for you, traditional returns do not apply. Instead, we offer a promise of flawless execution.

01. Guarantee

The Remake Policy

If your case arrives with a manufacturing defect, incorrect texture, or does not fit your specified phone model, we will remake it and ship it to you free of charge.

  • Defects must be reported within 7 days of delivery.
  • Photographic evidence of the issue is required for our craft team to review.
02. Exceptions

What We Cannot Return

As a bespoke studio based in Moldova, we cannot accept returns or offer remakes for:

Errors in Input

Incorrect initials or symbols provided during the ordering process.

Model Selection

Ordering for the wrong phone model (e.g., iPhone 15 instead of iPhone 15 Pro).

Aesthetic Preference

The "invisible" nature of black-on-black texture is our signature. We do not refund if you find the texture too subtle.

03. Process

How to Request a Remake

To initiate a review, please send your order number and clear photos of the item to our support channel. Our team typically responds within 24–48 hours.
