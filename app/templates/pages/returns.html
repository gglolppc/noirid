Our standard is perfection. Nothing less.

We want you to be fully satisfied with your Noirid experience. Because each piece is a bespoke creation tailored to your identity, we offer a Quality Guarantee focused on precision and flawless execution.

01. Policy

Our Commitment

If your product does not meet our high-quality standards due to manufacturing defects, physical damage, or a mismatch with the phone model specified, we provide a Free Remake or a full reimbursement.

  • Quality issues must be reported within 14 days of delivery (EU standard).
  • A video or high-resolution photo showing the defect is required for our team to initiate the resolution.
02. Returns

Personalized Items

In accordance with international consumer laws, items that are clearly personalized or custom-made are exempt from the standard right of withdrawal once production has begun.

Note on Aesthetic

The "Stealth" black-on-black texture is a signature of NOIRID. Its visibility is light-dependent by design. Please note that dissatisfaction with the "subtlety" of the design or customer-side errors (incorrect initials/phone model) do not qualify for a remake or refund.

03. Taxes

Zero-Tax Guarantee

We cover all import VAT for EU orders. If your local customs office requires a payment upon delivery, we will reimburse the full amount.

Simply pay the duty, email a photo of the receipt to support@noirid.com, and we will refund that exact amount back to your original payment method within 24 hours.

04. Support

How to Contact Us

Our team is here to assist with any quality reviews or tax refunds. We typically respond within 24–48 hours to ensure a swift resolution.
