Delivery

We ship internationally with tracked delivery and secure packaging. Orders are prepared within 1–2 business days and handed to our courier with a tracking number.

  • Standard shipping: 5–12 business days (international).
  • Express shipping: 3–6 business days (select destinations).
  • Tracking: emailed automatically once dispatched.

Customs duties or local taxes (if any) are paid by the recipient according to local regulations.
