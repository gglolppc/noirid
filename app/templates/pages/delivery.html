Shipping Flow

Every NOIRID piece is crafted to order in our Moldova studio and shipped worldwide with care. We prioritize precision, reliability, and transparency at every step.

01. Handling

2–4 Business Days

All orders are verified, handcrafted, and quality-checked before dispatch. We maintain small-batch production to ensure every texture and fit meets our standards—no rush, no shortcuts.

02. Transit Time

Priority: Romania
3–7 Working Days

Express delivery via Nova Poshta. Fast and reliable shipping directly from our studio to your door.

Rest of Europe
5–15 Working Days

Standard international shipping via reliable postal services. Fully tracked from dispatch to delivery.

*Please note: Each case is custom-textured individually. Production takes 2-4 business days before shipping.

Universal Tracking

A unique tracking number is emailed immediately upon handover to the courier. Full visibility from dispatch to delivery.

Real-time tracking included

03. Taxes & Guarantee

We believe in a seamless experience. As an independent studio, we don't collect VAT at checkout, but we won’t let you pay extra for our craft.

The Noirid VAT Refund:

If your local customs office requests a VAT payment upon delivery, simply pay it, send a photo of the receipt to support@noirid.com, and we will refund the full amount to your card within 24 hours.

*This guarantee ensures that the price you see on our site is the final price you pay, no matter where you are in the EU.
